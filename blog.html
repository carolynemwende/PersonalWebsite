Saiwa Swamp National Park

This is a place I woun't like to miss. Before I plan to go to spain as many aspire to,I must visit Saiwa. It is one of the smallest parks in kenya but full of incredible scenaries and peculier creatures. It is in saiwa that you will get to see the Sitatunga antelope which is semi aquatic and a great creature to always look at. What completes the place is the wondeful tree house present in the place and a place where you get to capture a nice view of the park.Take a tour to the saiwa and you will forget about going to spain.
